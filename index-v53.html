The Instrument
Prism is a mirror for your political instincts. Not a quiz. Not a score. A map — drawn by you, one event at a time — that reveals where you actually stand, and where the distance lives between what you believe and what you expect.
Swipe to continue →
The Interaction
Each day brings a prompt — a real event, framed without editorial. You choose one of four responses, then place a pin on the graph to mark your intensity. The words around you respond to your position. Say more if you want. The instrument is listening for depth.
Swipe to continue →
The Parallax
Pull the header down and the view shifts. The same data, seen from a different angle — aggregate patterns, interpretive filters, and the Diatribe: your running record of good faith. The more you return, the more the instrument reveals. Your depth is earned, not assigned.
